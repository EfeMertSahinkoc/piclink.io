Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about Piclink? You've come to the right place! Our FAQ section is designed to provide you with clear and concise answers to commonly asked questions, ensuring that you have all the information you need to make the most of our platform.

How do I sign up for Piclink?

Signing up for Piclink is quick and easy! Simply download the app from the App Store or Google Play Store, or visit our website and click on the "Sign Up" button. Follow the on-screen instructions to create your account by providing your email address, creating a password, and entering any other required information. Once your account is created, you can start sharing your photos instantly!

Is Piclink available on multiple devices?

Yes, Piclink is available on multiple devices. You can access Piclink on both iOS and Android devices by downloading the app from the respective app stores.

How can I share photos with friends and family on Piclink?

Sharing photos with friends and family on Piclink is simple. First, you create an event and upload your photos into it. Then, to share the event with others, you can generate a unique QR code associated with that event. Others can simply scan the QR code using the Piclink app to gain access to the event and view the photos.

Can I download my photos with Piclink?

Yes, you can download your photos with Piclink! We provide the option to download your photos directly from the app to your device. Whether you want to save your memories for offline viewing or share them on other platforms, downloading your photos with Piclink is quick and easy.
